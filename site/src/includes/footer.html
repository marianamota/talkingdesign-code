• About

    The designer is fundamentally a visual communicator. To get that right though we need to be able to receive a good brief, explain to clients, stakeholders and developers our rationale behind our design decisions, talk to users, facilitate workshops, give constructive feedback to other designers and get team members to buy in. These are all verbal forms of communication that come with visual support (sketches, flow diagrams, mock ups).

    The role of a designer today is not only to communicate visually but also to facilitate conversations and provide clarification to a whole project team. No design will go anywhere without good verbal communication.

    Mariana is a UX Designer with over 12 years of experience and Lead designer at software development company Oxford Computer Consultants. She co-organises UX Oxford.
